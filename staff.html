Meet Our Leaders

School Director

Dedicated Leadership

Our school leadership brings decades of experience in education and community development. Under their guidance, Lynpark Learning Centre has grown from a small initiative to a thriving institution serving hundreds of learners in the community.

JM

Mr. James Mwangi

Head Teacher

Mr. Mwangi has over 20 years of experience in education and has been leading LLC since 2013. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Education from Kenyatta University and is passionate about providing quality education to every child in rural Kenya.

GW

Mrs. Grace Wanjiku

Deputy Head Teacher

Mrs. Wanjiku brings 15 years of teaching experience and a Master's degree in Curriculum Development. She oversees academic programs and teacher development, ensuring the CBC curriculum is delivered with excellence.

Our Educators

Teaching Staff

A Team That Inspires

Our dedicated teachers are the heart of LLC. Each brings passion, expertise, and a genuine love for children. All our teachers hold relevant professional qualifications and undergo continuous training under the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

Together, they create a nurturing learning environment where every child is encouraged to discover their potential and thrive both academically and personally.
