About Our School

Welcome to Lynpark Learning Centre

Lynpark Learning Centre (LLC) is a registered primary school under the Ministry of Education, Kenya. Nestled in the heart of rural Kenya, we are committed to reaching the most vulnerable and needy children, providing them with world-class education that builds character and excellence.

Our dedicated team of educators delivers the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) from PP1 through Grade 8, ensuring every learner develops the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in the modern world. At LLC, we believe every child deserves an opportunity to be the best they can be.
