Begin Your Child's Journey

We welcome learners from PP1 to Grade 8. Our admission process is straightforward, supportive, and designed to help every family feel at home at LLC.

How To Apply

Application Process

1

Inquiry

Contact us by phone, email, or visit. We'll answer all your questions.

2

School Visit

Tour our campus, meet teachers, and see our facilities firsthand.

3

Application

Submit the application form with required documents.

4

Assessment

A simple, friendly assessment to understand your child's learning level.

5

Enrollment

Receive your offer letter, complete fees, and join the LLC family!

What You Need

Admission Requirements

Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2)

  • Child must be at least 4 years old (PP1) or 5 years old (PP2)
  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Immunisation records
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Parent/guardian national ID copy

Primary (Grade 1 — 6)

  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Previous school report card / progress report
  • Transfer letter from previous school (if applicable)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Medical records / health form

Junior Secondary (Grade 7 — 8)

  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Grade 6 assessment report / KPSEA results
  • Transfer letter and leaving certificate
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Parent/guardian contact information

General Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Non-refundable application fee
  • Willingness to abide by school rules and code of conduct
  • Parent commitment to attend school meetings and events
  • Medical fitness declaration
